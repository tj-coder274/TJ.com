The idea

What is TJ, exactly?

It started with videos. We were making them and just sitting on them in a private folder, doing nothing. One day it hit us — why keep all this to ourselves? I asked my brother Jenny, "why can't we put this out there for everyone to see?"

That conversation became TJ. We started with the inventions, then realized we were also screen-recording our gameplay and never sharing it — so TJ Gaming followed. More channels are already in the works, like TJ Reacts.

From private folders to public channels — TJ is everything me and Jenny were sitting on, finally out in the open.
